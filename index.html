About me

Annicca Albano is a Filipina creative who writes about life and lifestyle. Her works have appeared on Young Star by The Philippine Star, Cosmopolitan Philippines, and DG Traveler. In the past six years, she has dabbled in e-commerce copywriting, social media marketing, and email marketing. She currently resides in Canada, where she is pursuing a diploma in journalism.

Annicca took up Speech Communication at the University of the Philippines, authoring the first ethnography on spoken word in the country for her undergraduate thesis. After working as an associate editor for e-commerce startups, she made the leap into freelancing. She has also taken a 5-course specialization on Content Strategy for Professionals from Northwestern University and a course certificate on Brand Identity and Strategy from IE Business School.

A PCOS survivor and daughter of diabetic patients, Annicca uses her platform to share feel-good good food. In 2021, she and her mom opened a fast food franchise dedicated to Filipino comfort food.

When she's not writing from the heart, she's baking with the intention of making others feel loved.
